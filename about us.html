About Us

Our story, mission, and vision

Our Story

ALL SOUL'S WINNING FOR SAVIOUR GLOBAL MINISTRY began with a small group of devoted believers who shared a vision for bringing God's word to the community. What started as humble gatherings in homes has grown into a vibrant church family that continues to impact lives locally and globally.

Through the years, our church has remained committed to its founding principles of faith, love, and service. We've grown not just in numbers but in our ability to minister to the diverse needs of our congregation and community.

Today, under the leadership of REV'D James Oyegbile, we continue to build on this rich heritage while embracing new opportunities to share God's love with the world.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by faith, driven by purpose

Our Mission

To share the love of Christ, make disciples, and empower believers to transform their communities through faith in action. We are committed to spreading the Gospel message while meeting the spiritual, emotional, and physical needs of all people.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and transformation, creating a global community of believers who exemplify Christ's love in all aspects of life. We envision a church without walls, reaching across cultural and social boundaries to unite people in God's love.

Our Leadership

Guided by faith and committed to service

REV'D James Oyegbile

Senior Pastor

REV'D James Oyegbile has been faithfully leading ALL SOUL'S WINNING FOR SAVIOUR GLOBAL MINISTRY with wisdom, compassion, and dedication. His vision for the church has inspired countless individuals to deepen their faith and engage in meaningful service.

With a heart for teaching God's word and equipping believers for ministry, Pastor James continues to guide our congregation toward spiritual growth and effective community impact.

Our church is blessed with dedicated elders, deacons, and ministry leaders who work alongside REV'D James Oyegbile to shepherd our congregation and implement our vision.
