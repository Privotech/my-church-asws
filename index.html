Welcome to All Soul’s Winning for Saviour Global Ministry,
Greetings in the name of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ

We are delighted to welcome you to "All Soul’s Winning for Saviour Global Ministry", a place where God's love, grace,
and power are made manifest. Our mission is to win souls for Christ, disciple believers, and empower people to live victorious
lives through the Word of God and the power of the Holy Spirit.

At ASWS Global Ministry, we believe that every soul matters to God, and it is our passion to spread the gospel to the ends of the earth.
Whether you are joining us for the first time or are a committed member of our faith family, we want you to know that you are loved,
valued, and welcome here. We invite you to worship with us, grow in faith, and experience the transforming power of God’s presence.
Feel free to explore our website, connect with us through our online platforms, and join us in our mission of spreading the message of
salvation.

May the Lord bless you abundantly, and may His grace be with you always.

OUR VALUES

BEING SENT

Being sent means actively engaging with our local
and global communities, meeting people where
they are, and sharing the love and hope of Jesus
through our words and actions.

J.O.Y

We seek to live out the transformative power of
J.O.Y. – Jesus, Others, You. By prioritizing Jesus
above all else, and then caring for others, we
empower ourselves to better serve our community
and grow in our faith walk.

WORSHIP

Our foundation is built on worship. We believe in
the power of lifting our voices and hearts to God,
celebrating His goodness, and encountering His
presence.
